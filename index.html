SHORT BIO

Johann Sebastian Bach was bon in 1685, in Thuringia, in a family full of musicians.

Bach was well-known by his contemporaries as an excellent harpsichordist, organist, and expert on organ building. But now is considered the GOAT of the composers.

His beautiful compositions shows his profound Christianism. In all of the eclesiastic wok of Bach and in some of his non-church works he wrote the initials: "S.D.G." which stand for "Soli Deo Gloria", this is a Latin term for Glory to God alone.(1)

One of the most remarkable piece of music written by Bach is the "St Matthew Passion", which is described as one of the best piece of music of all times.
